Tips for Turkish Market
1.	Timing Matters: Peak interest periods are:
o	August-September (school start)
o	December-January (first semester exams)
o	March-June (final sprint to YKS)
2.	Localization: Use authentic Turkish education terminology
3.	Trust Building: Highlight security, data privacy, and success rates
4.	Parent Outreach: Create content addressing parent concerns
5.	Mobile First: 90% of Turkish students use mobile devices for studying
